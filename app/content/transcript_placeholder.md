The next iteration of this app is a transcript analysis tool: upload an earnings call transcript as a PDF or TXT file, and Claude works through it to extract management's tone, identify forward-looking statements and the hedging language around them, and flag numeric guidance changes versus the prior quarter. The output is a one-page memo with citations back to the specific sections of the transcript so an analyst can verify each claim in seconds rather than re-reading the whole document. This is the most natural extension of the AI Data Chat section above — it reuses the same model, the same prompt scaffolding, and the same citation pattern — and would slot in without rearchitecting anything that already exists in this app.
